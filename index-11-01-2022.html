Happy new year and lets get ready to SSI! We have passed through the year of 2021 where:

  • EU said everyone was going to get a digital wallet within this decade
  • DIN delivered application for research(yet to be given a response)
  • Decentralized ID consortias is happening all over the world and we need one in Norway
  • The big US of A is working on important SSI collaborations, that can be highliy important for Norwegians attempt to solve digitization of the 7 life actions, as explained in our newsletter here
  • We in DIN are trying to put a focus on use cases and how we can be affected by a better identity infrastructure

Now its time to put the metal to the pedal and join under a bigger mission to provide a better identity infrastructure for the people of the nordics!

 

Follow our future events and share this newsletter so we can grow our knowledge and discussions that will help get closer to better data & identity control.
